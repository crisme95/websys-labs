What is the Company Expose?

The Company Expose is the largest ITWS recruitment event. It features students and recruiters engaging in casual games and activities. It's an excellent oppurtunity for students to meet with employers and network with other students as well.

Newsletter

Gamma Nu Eta offers a newsletter as well. The newsletter is a once a year mailing that GNH sends to alumni updating them about the goings on in ITWS and RPI at large.

Tutoring

Gamma Nu Eta offers tutoring to its fellow ITWS students. They hold weekly tutoring sessions every Wednesday at Lally Hall.
